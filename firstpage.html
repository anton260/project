Frequent mistakes in the interior

Ill-conceived layout and ergonomics of space: it all starts with a planning solution. For example: without a clear plan, real items may simply not fit into the niche for which they are intended, which is often the case with built-in appliances and furniture. Incorrect zoning of space is manifested in the incorrect color designation of individual zones without taking into account the location of the window, door, mixing of several zones, where there is room for only one. The selection of a suitable color scheme is a fundamental factor in a beautiful interior design in Kharkiv, as it allows you to hide planning flaws and shortcomings in the architecture of the premises in Kharkiv. "Crooked" color schemes not only spoil the freshest and most beautiful design, but also expose all design flaws. Bust with interior decoration: for example, use parquet in the living room, laminate in the bedroom, linoleum in the hallway. A clear mix of finishing materials (this applies to both flooring and wall and ceiling finishes) will always be one of the most losing options. Complex multi-level ceilings have long gone out of fashion: today they no longer give originality to the interior, they only hide space, “crush” and smear any beautiful interior accents. The pursuit of trends for the sake of fashion and established stereotypes is an equally common mistake, which practically excludes one of the most important aspects of beautiful design - individuality, which should fully reflect the character, sense of beauty and the inner world of the owner of the house. The abundance of expensive branded items and fashionable art objects looks cool only at first glance, in fact, being the main indicator of bad taste and blind copying.

our work services

1.Interior Design

An integral part of the arrangement of the internal space. The main thing here is an integrated approach, covering not only the development of a design project and the choice of high-quality materials, but also following modern trends in space design. We offer turnkey interior design development - the service includes the creation of a sketch based on a measurement plan and the wishes of the customer, high-quality 3D visualization, development of a detailed plan, preparation of specifications for equipment, utilities, lighting, furniture and finishes. There is also an option with author's support - control of all repair and finishing works for compliance with the project. As a result, you will get a competent design, where everything is harmonious and functional.

2.Exterior Design

A good presentation of an architectural project increases its cost several times! This is a great solution to give your home a personality and make it more stylish! The service includes the development of the design of the facade and the surrounding area, sketch zoning of the site and visualization in several versions, which allows the customer to choose the most successful option. We draw up a complete specification of all the necessary works and decorative and finishing materials, work out the color concept, the location of lighting fixtures, paths and other exterior elements, so that the design turns out to be really convenient and stylish.

3.Implementation

The implementation of a construction project is always a complex but interesting process, and the main condition for its successful completion is the participation of a professional construction team. Masters who know all the construction processes, use new technologies and modern materials, perform the task efficiently and on time. We will make a comprehensive turnkey repair, including all the necessary steps: leveling walls and ceilings (plaster, primer, putty), floor screeding and laying flooring, laying water supply and drainage pipes, installing underfloor heating and creating a heating system, dismantling/installation partitions and electrical wiring. Everything that is needed for the implementation of the project - from start to finish - will be done quickly and in accordance with all the rules!
